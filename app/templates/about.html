About

Data Source

The data used to complete this project was scraped from Skiresort.info.com

Ski Resort Details

The ski resort detail have basic information about each of the resorts including the terrain details and pricing.

Ratings

The website also provides ratings that have been put together by ski experts who have done onsite visits at each of the resorts. The categories included in the evaluation are ski-related and non ski-related.

(Half)Pipeline for Analysis

All the data was merged together and cleaned for further analysis.

Content-Based Filtering

Content-based filtering determines the cosine similarity scores of all of the ski resorts. Upon receiving information about a resort that a use likes, the pairwise similarity scores are calculated and the top 10 are reported.

Collaborative Filtering

A collaborative filtering approach relies on user reviews to make recommendations. The model learns what resorts users are likely to enjoy given that they like similar resorts to other users. In the future, I plan to build a database to be able to collect user reviews and improve recommendations further.
